In 1999, Sony Corp. released the AIBO, one of the world's first commerically avalible entertainment robots. Throughout a 15 year lifespan, the iconic series of robotic dogs created a foundation for the future of robotics and forged a community that thrives to this day.

AIBO's are well built robots but they are not without their faults. The 16 degrees of freedom that make up AIBO comes with a price- after prolonged periods of time, joints tend to jitter and restrain movement. This is typically curable with a healthy dose of movement!

AIBO THERAPY was created to resolve these issues with minimal human interaction with movements streamlined to get your AIBO working as quickly as possible.
