Q: How do I get AIBO to start moving?

A: State out loud in a clear voice which mode you would like to begin. Full body- "ACTION ONE." Head only- "ACTION TWO." Legs only- "ACTION THREE."

Q: My AIBO stopped moving after a while. Now what?

A: Touch your AIBO's head to begin the hour long cycle again. Press the pause button to let your AIBO get some rest.

Q:Help! My AIBO is making grinding sounds!

A: If it seems like your robot is having serious problems, turn it off immediatley and try to *gently* move the joint manually. If there are grinding sounds (light motor noise is normal) it may be time for a trip to the vet.
