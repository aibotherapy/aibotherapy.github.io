AIBO THERAPY STAND
An AIBO stand design has been created and was designed to eliminate all stress on the motors during AIBO THERAPY. If you plan to run AIBO THERAPY for long portions of time, creating one of these stands could be extremely useful. You can also use a Sony display stand if you are lucky enough to find one.
